Have you always wanted to create at home the same experience you get while drinking coffee made by your favorite Barista?

Have you ever been frustrated after making the effort of buying a coffee machine, or beans from your local Deli and it still didn't taste right?

We are here to help! Di Bartoli is an expert in the Home Barista field and is passionate about empowering Coffee lovers to achieve coffee excellence at home.

We help you taking the guesswork out of divine home coffee and support you every step of the way with a professional advice, quality gear, technical support, coffee beans choices and naturally, training.
We are honoured and grateful to have been working with over 10,000 home baristas in Australia for the last 10 years.

Today, some of them who have partnered with us for several years, share their experiences below so that you too can realize help is accessible and easy. You just need to reach out a hand and engage with us!

Whether you want to learn more about improving your home coffee, buying new gear, attending a workshop, understanding Specialty Coffee better, or just seeking to impress your coffee lover significant other. We are the only one-stop-shop retailer, educator, and roaster in Australia that is dedicated to take you from confusion to clarity and from mediocre to sublime at home, answers and lots of fun are to be found right here.
